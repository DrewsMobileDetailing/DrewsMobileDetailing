Our Story

Drew’s Mobile Detailing began as a one-man hustle with a bucket and a dream. Founder Andrew recognized a need for high-quality detailing services that were convenient, flexible, and built on trust. What started with one satisfied customer has grown into a reliable team serving people and businesses across Lake, Cook, and McHenry counties.

We bring our tools, passion, and attention to detail straight to you — whether you're at home, work, or preparing for a special event. Our mission is simple: to leave every vehicle looking its absolute best, every time.
